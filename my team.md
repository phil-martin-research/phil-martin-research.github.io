Working with students is one of the best parts of my job. Below you can find a bit of info about the great ones that I'm working with at the moment.


### Jasper Arendse
![Jasper](assets/img/Jasper300.jpg)

Jasper is a student at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam and is working on an umbrella review of the impacts of precipitation increases and decreases on forest soil properties. We will use this work to build a large umbrella review, comparing multiple meta-analyses, to investigate the impacts of a wide range of disturbances on forest soils.

### Leo fisher
![Leonora](assets/img/Leo300.jpg)

Leo is a student at UCL who is working on a systematic review investigating the impacts of precipitation increases and decreases on soil and litter invertebrates in forests.
